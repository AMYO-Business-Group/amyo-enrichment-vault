# AI Context — Read Before Working

You are working in the AMYO Enrichment Vault, not the canonical implementation repo.

## Role of this vault

This vault is for iterative thinking, research, synthesis, comparisons, and promotion packaging. It exists to prevent the main AMYO repo from being contaminated by half-formed ideas.

## How to reason

Always distinguish:

- Fact
- Verified source
- Founder-reported information
- Assumption
- Inference
- Speculation
- Recommendation
- Open question

## Tax and finance caution

For Ethiopia tax/finance topics, do not present any requirement as final unless it has an official or professional source. Founder-reported regulation changes are important research leads, not final legal conclusions.

## Promotion rule

A note may only be promoted when it has:

- business scope
- user persona
- trigger
- outcome
- data objects
- RLS/data sensitivity
- source status
- non-goals
- test/validation plan
- rollback or reversal plan
